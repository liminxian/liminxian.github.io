Biography

Jingdong Wang is a Senior Researcher at Visual Computing Group, Microsoft Research Asia. His areas of interest include efficient CNN architecture design, person re-identification, large-scale indexing, and salient object detection. He has been serving/served as an Associate Editor of IEEE TMM and IEEE TCSVT, and an area chair (or SPC) of some prestigious conferences in vision, multimedia, and AI, such as CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, ACM MM, IJCAI, and AAAI. He is a Fellow of IAPR. CV
